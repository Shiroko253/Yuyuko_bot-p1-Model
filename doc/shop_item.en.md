## 🔋 Stamina System

*   **Max Stamina**: 20 points.
*   **Work Cost**: Using `/work` consumes 2 ~ 8 stamina depending on your job.
*   **Recovery**: Buy food using `/shop`, or eat items from your `/backpack`.
*   **Pricing**: Item Price = `Base Price + (Base Price * Tax %)`.
