## 🌊 Fishing Scenarios & Passives

Depending on your job, your fishing experience will be completely different:

*   **Ordinary Jobs**: A gentle breeze blows across the Cherry Blossom Lake. You sit on the shore and leisurely cast your rod. This is the most comfortable afternoon in the Netherworld.
*   **Fisherman (Main Job)**: You drive a small wooden boat into the deep sea. The sea wind howls, and waves slap against the hull. This is true nautical fishing! *(Passive: Rare catch rate greatly increased)*
*   **Angler (Sub Job)**: You arrive at the "Big Catch" secret realm with treacherous undercurrents! Gripping your rod tightly, ready to tug-of-war with the leviathans below! *(Passive: 30% chance of returning empty-handed)*
