## 💼 Job System Overview

In Yuyuko's Netherworld Garden, souls must work to earn **👻 Ghost Coins**.
We use an advanced **"Main Job + Sub Job" dual-track system**:
*   **Main Job**: Determines your salary range and **Stamina Cost** when working.
*   **Sub Job**: Currently, only "Angler" is available. It doesn't affect your work but triggers a special passive when fishing.

> ⚠️ **Shiroko's Reminder**: Working costs Stamina! When your stamina hits zero, you must buy food from `/shop` or eat from your `/backpack`, or you'll just be staring at the cherry blossoms instead of working.

## ✨ Passive Skills Explained

### 1. Gambler's Resolve
*   **Applicable Command**: `/blackjack` (Single-player 21-point)
*   **Mechanic**:
    *   Every bet automatically deducts **3x** the base amount.
    *   If you **WIN**, you receive a **6x** total payout (Net profit: 3x).
    *   If you **TIE**, your 3x deducted principal is refunded.
    *   If you **LOSE**, you lose the entire 3x principal.
*   *Shiroko's comment: Win big or go broke. That's the romance of a gambler.*

### 2. Fisherman's Intuition
*   **Applicable Command**: `/fish`
*   **Mechanic**: Greatly increases the chance of catching rare fish.
    *   **Legendary** chance increased to **3x**.
    *   **Deify** chance increased to **5x**.
    *   **Unknown** chance increased to **4x**.
*   *Yuyuko: The treasures of the deep sea can only be found by the bravest fishermen~*

### 3. Angler's Obsession
*   **Applicable Command**: `/fish`
*   **Mechanic**:
    *   Every cast has a **30% chance of returning empty-handed (Skunked)**.
    *   But while waiting by the water, you seem to encounter legendary big catches more often...
*   *Shiroko's comment: An angler's destiny is to be accompanied by empty-handedness~*
